Just because I don't have enough going on in my life right now, I decided to start up a new blog. My RSS feed is full of craft and sewing blogs that I follow. Lately, my head concocted a bunch of craft ideas and the desire to submerge myself in the crafting/sewing community. Leading to the new blog Simply Modern Mom.

Simply Modern Mom is a blog where I post my projects and ideas. It's a place to collaborate on ideas. And a place to share inspirations.

Since my Simply Modern Mom blog is public, I have changed the names of my children and removed any information giving away my location. Please keep it that way.

But please hop over there and check it out. Leave lots of comments and put it on your RSS feed. Check the archives for past posts. I just started it yesterday, so not too much going on there.

Also follow it on Twitter.

And don't worry, this blog will stay the same. This is where you can read all about what we're doing. There will be updates constantly. So keep coming back.
