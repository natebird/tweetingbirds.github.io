Laney loves to tell knock knock jokes. Especially when I tell her "No, no," for something she is not supposed to be doing. She responds with, "Knock-knock," and proceeds to tell a joke. The knock-knock jokes usually are in jibberish ending with her laughing out loud. The other day she actually said one that made sense.

Laney: Knock-knock.

Tiffany: Who's there?

Laney: Eyes. [pointing to her eyes]

Tiffany: Eyes who?

Laney: Eye see you. Bahahaha.
