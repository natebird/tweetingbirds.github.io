Laney: Had her 2 week checkup today. She is one healthy baby. She has gained a little more than 2 pounds since birth, weighing at 10 pounds now. She will be three weeks old tomorrow. She does have a bit of acid reflux, but we are mainly keeping her more upright to help with that. She is already feeding every 3-4 hours and letting me sleep 4 hour shifts at night. I love sleeping babies! She is having a hard time falling asleep during the day for naps because she wants to nurse to sleep. I'm trying to get her to fall asleep on her own without any sleep aids.

She is one who strives on routine and familiarity.
