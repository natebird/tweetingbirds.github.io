Last Saturday was an exciting day for us. My family threw a surprise party for my grandparent's 60th anniversary. Their anniversary was Monday, March 15. And my aunties, Joy and Lori had this in planning months before. I mean 60th anniversary is a big deal. Not many people reach 60th. So it deserved a huge celebration. They invited everyone and had quite a few people show up. People we haven't seen in years. It was a fun little reunion. 
The place was decorated with woodwork carpentry things my grandpa made and quilts my grandma made. She made a quilt for each family and great grand children. They have 21 great grand children. Also decorated with our family shirts. I think the family did a fantastic job, all working together to put this party together. And my grandparents had no idea. The surprise part was going to be a bit difficult considering the party was at their church building (across the street from their house). But their stake president was in on it and helped us get them their without suspicion. 
There was a short little program that brought many laughter and tears to all of us. We sang our family song, "I Am a Child of Bob." I am so blessed to be in such a wonderful family. And I love how we all live relatively close and get together often. Some of my best memories are with these wonderful people. It was also nice to see my cousin Paul who is serving in the Army. He got special permission to leave base for a few hours and drove a long ways to attend the party.
