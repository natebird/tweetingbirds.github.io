One thing I miss about Christmas time in Utah is not so much the snow, but the Christmas lights that go up at Temple Square each year. It's beautiful and a fun activity to visit Temple Square. They put up the life size nativity scene that narrates every half hour or so. Then they have other smaller nativity scenes set up around the reflection pond.
