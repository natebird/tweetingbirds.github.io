8 things I learned from my past

  1. It's hard to keep in touch with friends, but we all need to surround ourselves with good friends.
  2. Do what you are supposed to and you'll be fine.
  3. Nagging doesn't do much good. I'm still in the process of learning this one.
  4. Be positive and have faith. It influences people in a happy way.
  5. Listen more. Let others speak. Ask questions.
  6. Giving is better than receiving. I am still learning this one, too.
  7. It is OK to ask for help. It is OK to say no.
  8. Education is a valuable thing. There is much to learn from those older than me.
