I picked up Lai Lai from nursery.

Tiffany: How was nursery?
Lai Lai: It was so great! There were no boys in my nursery class. Only girls.
Tiffany: No boys in your class?
Lai Lai: Nope. They all disappeared. I was so happy. I even smiled!
