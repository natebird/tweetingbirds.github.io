Kenzie: Mom, I want you to read me a story again.

Tiffany: You want me to read you and story? What about dad?

Kenzie: I don't want daddy to read me a story.

Tiffany: Why don't you want daddy to read you a story?

Kenzie: Because some times he does it wrong and some times he does it right.

Tiffany: How does daddy read a story wrong?

Kenzie: He makes up the story.
