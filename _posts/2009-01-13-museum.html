After several attempts and rescheduling, we were finally able to attend the museum to see the Jim Henson's display. I had won a family pass to the museum back in October from a drawing. The display wasn't as kid friendly as we had hoped. It displayed mostly his artworks and drawing. Only a few puppets made it behind glass cases. However, there was a kid room at the end with activities - that was Kenzie's favorite part. We also spent some time in the gift shops looking at stuff.
