My parents decided to migrate to the South for Christmas. They needed sunshine and the beach. So off they went to Florida for a little over a week. At first I was like, "What?! You can't leave your family the week of Christmas!" But it actually turned out nice. We had a nice relaxing Christmas with just our little family of four for half the day. Then we spent the afternoon and evening with my extended family at Grandma and Grandpa's house. It's nice to have other family around so when my own parents decided to ditch us for Florida, we had other peeps to hang with.

Christmas Eve, we trekked over to my aunty Lori's house for her traditional Southern breakfast (this year it was for dinner). She makes the most delicious buttermilk biscuits, sausage patties, bacon, eggs all smothered in gravy. Oh. It was to die for. Then that night Laney started to get a low fever. Her ear drum in her left ear had ruptured the week before and had been draining ever since. It became infected but I couldn't get her to the doctor until the following Monday morning. She was a miserable little girl. But such good sport for making through all the Christmas festivities.
