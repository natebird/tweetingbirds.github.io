This is what I heard over the baby monitor this morning when Kenzie woke up, "I didn't eat my dinner and Mommy put my food away. I was crying. Then I couldn't eat dessert." Funny how the first thing she thinks of in the morning is how Mommy disciplined her last night for not eating her dinner.

After a while, she opened her door and came into our room. Obviously hungry because she didn't eat her dinner last night, this was the conversation we had.

Kenzie: Come on mom, let's go downstairs and eat some food.
Tiffany: Can I eat Kenzie?
Kenzie: No. We eat food.
Tiffany: But I want to eat Kenzie.
Kenzie: No. I'm not food. I'm a princess. I make princess noises and I do princess things.
