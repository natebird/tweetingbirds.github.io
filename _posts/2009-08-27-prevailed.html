Kenzie has been asking all day for mom or dad to get her drums from the attic. No luck.

When she was playing with Laney, Laney pulled her hair.

Kenzie: [coming to mom and dad crying] Laney messed up my Mulan hair. She pulled it.
Tiffany: She didn't mean to. She is still learning.
Kenzie: Now I'm sad because she messed up my Mulan hair. I need you to get my drums from the attic so I can be happy again.
Tiffany: How about I just fix your hair?

Nate, the good dad, got her drums for her. She's happy again.
