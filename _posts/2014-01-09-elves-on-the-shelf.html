This year we had two elves show up at our house. The girls named them Cole and Clara. They did some fun stuff around the house, even made big messes. I forgot to take pictures of some of the days. But one day they were roasting marshmallows over a candle and Laney ate their marshmallows. The later that same day they were gone because she touched them. They were gone for a few days until Kenzie helped Laney write a letter saying they were sorry for touching them. Then they came back. Another day they brought fake snow to the main nativity and they made snow angels in it.
