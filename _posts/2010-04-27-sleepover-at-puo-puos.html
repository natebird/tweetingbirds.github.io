March 18 was Kenzie's first sleepover ever. All on her own. At my parents' house. I did great. She did great. My parents were wonderful. My dad was a bit nervous. He didn't want her to wake up in the middle of the night, freak out and would have to bring her back home. I wasn't worried about that at all.

Kenzie had so much fun that she ended up staying there a second night. When my mom brought her home after the second night, she said that was fun. But next time Kenzie will only be spending one night because she just wore my mom out.

What a big girl.
