The local public broadcasting company hosted a family health day at their office building. It was next door to Nate's school downtown and a couple blocks away from Ikea. So we made the trip down to the event, telling Kenzie it was a surprise.

As we approached the building, there were big colorful signs with a guy standing outside blowing huge bubbles. Kenzie saw the guy got excited, "Wooooo!" When Nate parked the car in the parking deck, Kenzie kept asking, "Where my surprise go?"

The event was packed full of parents, grandparents, and CHILDREN! They provided craft stations throughout the building, a petting zoo, fresh apples and green beans from a local farmer's market, face paintings, clowns with balloons, storytelling, short plays from Bob the Builder, and characters from kids' favorite PBS shows - Clifford the Big Red Dog, Curious George, Wyatt and Princess Presto from Super Why. The best part of it all, it was free! And she came home with a gift bag full free books, kid tattoos, stickers, coloring pages, coupons to The Children's Museum and the History Museum, and all her crafts.

Kenzie had such a great time, even though as we were there, she was a bit overwhelmed with all the people and kids everywhere. And she was a bit concerned with the screaming kids throwing tantrums throughout the place. But when we got home, she kept ranting about making crafts, petting animals, and meeting Wyatt from Super Why and Smokey the Bear in person. Then we hopped over to Ikea for lunch and to grab a Lack shelf for the entry way.
