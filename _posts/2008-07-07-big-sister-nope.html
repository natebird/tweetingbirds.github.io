Earlier last week…

Tiffany: Kenzie, do you want to be a big sister?
Kenzie [with no hesitation]: No! Mommy, daddy, Kenzie Wenzie.
Tiffany: Do you want a baby sister?
Kenzie: No!
Tiffany: How about a baby brother?
Kenzie: No! Mommy, daddy, Kenzie Wenzie.

I think she knows that her world is about to be turned upside down having to share attention.

Yesterday, after church…

Kenzie: Mommy, daddy, Kenzie Wenzie, and baby sister. My family.

We want another girl, so I've been trying to tell Kenzie to wish for a baby sister. Now I hope we do have a girl so she doesn't get confused.
