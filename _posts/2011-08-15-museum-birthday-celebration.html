Kenzie loves going to museums. I love that about her. I wanted to instill this love for culture and museums in my children at a young age. So for Kenzie's birthday we went to Tellus Museum. We were surprised at how nice it was. And the girls had a great time. We didn't have much time to spend because we got there around 3 p.m. and they closed at 5 p.m. But we got to see everything and do the activities, just a bit rushed. Thanks to the Bank of America Museum on Us program, we only had to pay for Kenzie and the rest of us were free.

Afterwards we went over to my parents for dinner and for them to celebrate Kenzie's birthday. Since her birthday was on a school night, we tried to do most of the celebrating last Saturday.
