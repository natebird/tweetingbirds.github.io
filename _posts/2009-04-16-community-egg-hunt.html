We took the girlies to a community egg hunt in a nearby town. It was at a stake park and was surprisingly not too crowded. They had egg hunts according to age. Kenz, her cousin Zibby, and her friend Noah were in the 0-4 year-olds group. It was nice to have the age groups, but some of the parents were grabbing eggs for their kids, too. Kenzie got so excited when they said go that she ran past four eggs to get out in the middle of the field. Then she looked around and was able to pick up four eggs before they were all taken.

Along with the egg hunt, she played mini golf and egg toss. She did a cake walk and had her face painted, which she loved because Mulan also had her face painted in the movie. Laney slept through the whole thing.
