Kenzie knows her birthday is coming up. And she is excited about it's arrival. Lately, we have been having this same conversation just about every day.

Kenzie: My birthday is coming soon.
Tiffany: Yes it is. Do you know when it is?
Kenzie: August 9th.
Tiffany: That's right.
Kenzie: But it's not here yet. I have to wait 5 minutes, then it'll be my birthday.
