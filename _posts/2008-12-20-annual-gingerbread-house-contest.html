It's not really a contest since no one goes home a loser. We are all too nice to actually judge the houses and pick one above the rest. Instead, we all claim our own house to be the best and do a lot of trash talking. We are a family of trash talkers.

Nate and I started the gingerbread house tradition the first Christmas of our marriage. When we visited my parents for Christmas that year and we decided to do a FHE with an activity. This was the activity. We have been doing it every year since (I think we missed one year).
