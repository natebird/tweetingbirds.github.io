Here are some images from my life as a mom. I wouldn't trade this job for anything in the world. And I am so blessed to have the opportunity to stay at home with my girls. I adore them and love them more than anything. So here's a glimpse of my life, sometimes not so pretty, but all moments I want to remember because I know I will miss one day…

The explosive poopy diaper on a kid that won't stay still. Yikes…

The mess the kids can make right after you cleaned up…

The crazy schedules of keeping up with 5 people…

Finding a jar of pickles rolling around under your driver seat in the minivan and not knowing how long it had been there…

Making shirts and skirts and treat bags and games and a bunch of other stuff for school events, special dress up days, parties and so forth…

Playing games with the girls and realizing they will never know what a telephone was. Laney thought it was a clock…

Orders and purchases aren't so fun with stuff for me any more. It's all baby or kids stuff like diapers and booster seat…

Clipping coupons and looking for deals to make the family money last a little farther. Although I don't do much couponing any more on groceries, those coupons stinks…

Laundry day consists of me doing 4-6 loads of laundry. It also means I spend most of my time sorting and removing stains from clothes…
