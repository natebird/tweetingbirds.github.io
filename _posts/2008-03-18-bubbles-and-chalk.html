It was another beautiful, warm, sunny day. Kenzie and I spend part of the day outside after her afternoon nap. First, we just went out to blow some bubbles. It was quite the challenge for me to try to take pictures while blowing bubbles without getting myself or the camera drenched in bubble solution. Then, I decided to surprise Kenzie early with her Easter gift, a box of sidewalk chalk. It was such a nice day out that I thought it would be a great day to create art on the cement slab in our backyard. It's the perfect canvas for sidewalk art. Here are just a "few" pictures of our fun day outside.

As you can see, Kenzie has great stick people drawing skills and penmanship.

If you click on this picture to enlarge it, concentrate really hard, and squint, you will be able to see the bubbles flying to the left and above Kenzie.

Stretching out to better chase and catch the bubbles.

OK, you can actually see the bubbles in this picture! It was quite difficult to blow bubbles with one hand while trying to aim and take a picture with the other.

Kenzie pretended the bubble container was a phone. She went on chatting for about 5 minutes on that "phone" about being outside and chasing bubbles. I think she was "talking" to daddy because I heard his name come up a few times. But then I also heard the words mommy, Puo Puo, Dude, and Grandma. So it could have been any of those people. Apparently, this is also how I talk on my cell phone, I tuck it under my ear, because she copies whatever I do.

In her words, "Bubbles up!"

Here she is pretending the bubble container is a football and she is running through tackles. Well, at least that's what it looks like she is doing.
