Nate and I are gaming people- board games, strategy games. We had game nights with friends all the time (life before kiddos). For Christmas the first year of our marriage I got Nate the game Acquire. It's one of our favorite games of all time.

We played often with our friends, Grant and Kelly. They were big into Acquire also. On their turns, Nate and Grant would take a long time figuring out strategies and try to count everyone's hotel cards. Kelly and I were just random players, no strategies involved. We stayed up long hours into the night having tournaments. We even kept a chart of our winnings.
