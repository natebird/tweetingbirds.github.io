Kenzie at 38 months…

  • Ever so lovely. She looks so grown up in all her pictures. And she has an attitude of a 5-year-old.
  • Thinks she is always right.
  • Curious about everything.
  • Loves to learn and her mind absorbs everything.
  • Would live off peanut butter and jelly sandwiches and mac & cheese if I would let her. Good thing I make her try new foods. She actually discovered she likes zucchinis and squash.
  • Still obsessed with Disney Princesses. Knows all of their songs.
  • Memorized two disks of the Children's Primary Songbook songs.
  • Can quote the entire movies of Madagascar, Shrek, Shrek 2, Mulan, The Little Mermaid, The Bee Movie, and Aladdin.
  • Singing all day long, even makes up her own songs.
  • Getting a little better at sharing and being nice to her sister. The taking away Kenzie's toys for not being nice has worked.
  • Still goes down for quite time for two hours whether she naps or not.
  • Learning to read words by sounding out her alphabets.
  • Loves doing preschool workbooks. I'm looking into a little of homeschool preschool stuff with her.

Laney at 8 months…
  • Is a mover and a shaker. Crawls everywhere.
  • Pulls up on furniture and walking along them.
  • Does not like to be held unless she is facing out and looking at everything.
  • Is not a cuddler any more. Makes me so sad.
  • Loves nursing. I'm worry about having to wean her. She's not going to like that.
  • Such a happy little girl. Always smiling at everyone, even when she is sick.
  • Still sucking away at that thumb.
  • Grinds her teeth, yes, those front ones. It drives me insane.
  • Popped out tooth #5 (top left) with #6 not far behind.
  • Loves to clap and do the motions to this Chinese nursery rhyme my mom taught her.
  • Gobbles down yogurt, rice puffs, and crackers.
  • Drinks from a cup.
  • Takes two 2-hour naps and sleeps 11 hours through the night.
