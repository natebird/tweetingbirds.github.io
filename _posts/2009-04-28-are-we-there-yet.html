On our way to Red Robins for our birthday meal.

Kenzie: Mom, where are we going?
Tiffany: A restaurant.
Kenzie: Why are we going to a restaurant?
Tiffany: To eat.
Kenzie: What are we eating?
Tiffany: Food.
Kenzie: Mom, what are you doing?
Tiffany: Sitting in a car.
Kenzie: Why are you sitting?
Tiffany: To go somewhere.
Kenzie: Where are we going?

After about 20 minutes straight of questioning from Kenzie while riding in the car, we were all getting restless. And ignoring her didn't work.

Tiffany: [annoyed] Kenzie, you are talking a lot.
Kenzie: I like to talk.
Tiffany: I think you're just making stuff up to say.
Kenzie: I know I am.
