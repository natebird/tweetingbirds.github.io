Another Labor Day weekend activity. Thanks to the Museum on Us program with Bank of America, we toured the botanical gardens for free. It turned out to be a family photo shoot. We're going back again next month because after being there for three hours we only saw the children's section of the garden. Too much to see and so much to do. The kids loved it and the adults loved it. I really wanted to see all the Henry Moore sculptures. I only saw two. Definitely going back.

The day was overcast and a bit drizzly. At least it made the temperature and humidity bearable. And the photos turned out decent as long as I overexposed everything. And being the photographer, I didn't get any pictures of me. Oh well, maybe when we go there again next month.
