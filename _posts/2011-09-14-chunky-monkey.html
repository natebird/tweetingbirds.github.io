Took this adorable little girl to the pediatrician for her check up yesterday and she weighed in at 10 pounds 12 ounces at three weeks old. Good to know that I'm feeding her well.

So, I am finally producing enough milk to feed her solely on nursing. Yay. No more supplementing and pumping and nursing that took an hour to do. Now we can just nurse and be done. I am so grateful that the prescription worked and was able to increase my milk supply. Phew.

Her blocked tear duct is getting better. It is still blocked, but the pediatrician told me I can massage it to help unblock it. It's not as gooey as before. Thank goodness.
