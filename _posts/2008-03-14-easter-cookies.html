Today, a rainy day, Kenzie and I made Easter cookies. She had a blast cutting out letters spelling her name, rolling the cookie dough, eating the cookie dough, and decorating the cookies with yummy frosting. Some cookies were too delicious to finish decorating… hence the half eaten cookies in the batch. I frosted the cookies and did the red decorations, Kenzie poured the sprinkles on each of the cookies and covered her fingers in frosting and sprinkles to lick.
