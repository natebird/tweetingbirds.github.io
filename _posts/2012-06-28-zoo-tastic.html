One thing I wanted to do this summer was to take the girls to the zoo. We had been before but Laney was a newborn when we last went. This time the two older girls really enjoyed it. Lai Lai couldn't keep up and ended up falling asleep in the stroller, which was prefect for the rest of us so we could do more. The girls' favorite part was the carousel at the end. And they got to taste cotton candy for the first time. They also had fun finding the animals in each exhibit. We got to see all the animals they wanted to see. It was nice that the zoo let us bring food in because we packed our lunch and ate it halfway through the zoo. And the best part was we had a free pass to the zoo from the local library. Great day for all of us.
