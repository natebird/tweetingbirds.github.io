Kenzie's wish finally came true. She had been wishing and praying for snow since winter came around. We finally got hit with snow and extremely chilly weather that first week in January. The lows were in the 10's and the highs were in the 20's. I am not made for this kind of weather. I like it warm around the 70's all year round. Maybe that is because I grew up in Hawaii where it was warm in the 70's all year round.

Fun fact: When I moved from Hawaii to Georgia at age 14, I didn't own a pair of pants. I only wore shorts.

Anyway, the snow came down on Thursday, Jan. 7. Nate had a class for Six Sigma. He was gone. I surprised Kenzie with the snow outside our window around 1 p.m. She demanded to go outside after lunch. I bundled the girls. And out we went.
