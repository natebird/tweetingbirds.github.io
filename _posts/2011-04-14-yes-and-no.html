Laney's favorite phrase to say is "Um… No, thank you." That's her answer for just about anything- brush her teeth, eating dinner, picking up toys, bedtimes, etc. Nate decided to take advantage of this…

Nate: Laney, do you want to eat your food?

Laney: Um… No, thank you.

Nate: Laney, would you like a car when you get older?

Laney: Um… No, thank you.

Nate: Laney, do you want to date boys?

Laney: [without hesitation] Yes!

Nate: [stunned] What?! Laney, do you want to kiss boys?

Laney: [without hesitation] Yes!

Nate: That didn't go as planned.
