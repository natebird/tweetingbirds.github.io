As mentioned before, we went to the zoo last Saturday. It's a good thing we got in for free because we only spent a total of 1 1/2 hours in there. We skipped about half of the zoo when Kenzie was done having seen most of the animals from the movie, Madagascar. Kenzie's favorite were the pink flamingos. She also enjoyed the pandas.

On the way home, Kenzie had a little disturbance in the car when she realized she didn't see Gloria the hippo at the zoo. Very sad and pretended to cry, Kenzie was concerned that Gloria wasn't with the rest of the Madagascar gang at our zoo. We finally convinced her that Gloria was still at the San Diego zoo, part of the movie plot.
