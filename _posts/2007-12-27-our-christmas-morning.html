I hope everyone had a wonderful and memorable Christmas. We sure had fun opening gifts with Kenzie now that she understands what is happening. Here are the highlights of our Christmas morning. We didn't open gifts until about 11 a.m. because Kenzie slept in until 10 a.m. and we didn't get her out of bed for another half hour. It took all of us a while to open gifts, not because we had so many, but because Kenzie didn't care to open any after she found my new Chapsticks in my stocking. That was all she wanted and she played with them all morning. She did enjoy her new large Doodle Pad she got from us. Nate and I had a bunch of little gifts for each other since our computer was considered the big family gift this year, even though we opened it about a month ago. Enjoy the pictures!

Coming down the stairs Christmas morning. She wouldn't let Nate change her diaper because he told her Santa came.


Picked up her first present to open. She knew it was from Santa because it had his picture on the wrapping paper.


Helping Kenzie open her present.


Once she opened a present, she didn't care much about what else was happening.


She found my new Chapsticks. That was all she wanted for Christmas. She went around putting Chapstick on her lips and on mommy and daddy's lips the rest of the morning.


Kenzie did get excited about her new Doodle Pad that comes with magnetic shapes in a pull out drawer. She drew pictures for us all day.
