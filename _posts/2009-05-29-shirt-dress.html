In my "spare" time, when I neglect my children and house, I have been working on turning dress shirts into sundresses for my girlies. A lot of trial and errors trying to figure out the pattern for my girlies. One is tall and skinny and the other is short and chunky. And since I'm not the expert seamstress, I made numerous mistakes on this first dress. I'm hoping by the fourth one I will be able to do it with my eyes closed.
