Last year, my mom found a turtle sandbox for Kenzie. We haven't filled it with sand. But I thought it would be fun to fill it up with water and have a little backyard pool. The kids had a great time playing together. Laney fully submerged underwater twice and didn't shed a tear. Luckily I was right there to pick her up. The first time Kenzie pulled a toy out of Laney's hand, but since Laney had a tight grip on the toy, she got pulled under. The second time she was reaching for a toy, leaned too forward and lost balance.
