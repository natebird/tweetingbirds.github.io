  • She acts like such a big girl. Wants to do everything her sister is doing.
  • I can't get a picture of her anymore without it being burry because she moves to fast. 
  • She loves to try on shoes and wear everyone's shoes. 
  • She loves to sing. 
  • Loves to wear necklaces.
  • She doesn't say many words - mama, dada, ball, hi and bye. But she will blabber on and on. 
  • She does things her own way and on demand. 
  • When you tell her no, she will do it anyway. No turns it into a game for her. And she can shake her head no.
  • She loves to climb and get on top of everything. Very good at making me nervous.
  • She grunts and points at what she wants.
  • She only gives babies kisses. Until yesterday. She gave me a kiss.
  • She loves to give her big sister hugs. 
  • When we put her to bed, Kenzie and I sing her a song and talk about what Laney is going to dream about. 
  • She is fast at moving and running places.
  • Her toes are exactly like Nate's. 
  • She gained 2 pounds in 3 months- that was at her last check up. Weighs 21 pounds and 30" tall.
  • She knows how to open pens and markers. She loves to draw and write like Kenzie and me.
  • Very attached to me. Wants me to hold her all the time.
  • Very curious about everything. Wants to touch everything.
  • Will eat just about everything except baby food, meat sticks, hotdogs, sliced bread. 
  • Can go up and down the stairs by herself.
