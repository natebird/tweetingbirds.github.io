Kenzie has been saying it all the time: "It's so delicious!" It's the biggest word she knows. All of a sudden, "Yummy" has become a word for those less articulate. So yesterday, when we were making lemon cupcakes, she had something to say about that.

First, I let her lick the batter off the mixer beater. Then I left the kitchen to get the camera.
When I got back with the camera, I found her doing this with the comment, "Mommy, this is so delicious!"
After the baking and frosting process, I gave her 2 small cupcakes to devour. She ate them then decided to lick the frosting off her plate. All the while saying, "Mommy, this is delicious!"
She finally had to use her fingers to get the frosting she couldn't get off by licking the plate
