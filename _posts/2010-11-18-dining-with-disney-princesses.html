As part of the deal we got with our Disney package, we received a $300 gift card to use anywhere at Disney. It was great because we could purchase souvenirs for the kids without getting out our wallet. And we used part of the gift card to pay for the princess breakfast we had at Epcot. It was our surprise to the girls. Not that going to Disney World wasn't a surprise enough.

We dressed the girls up and they loved it. And of course I took some pictures of them.

The restaurant that provided the delicious but also expensive buffet breakfast. Luckily ours was paid for.

Upon entering the restaurant, you meet Belle and do photos with her. They switch up the princesses all the time. Laney loves Belle.

Then you sit and eat. Eat until you are full or until your children start to destroy the place. We experienced both.

The food was definitely European inspired because the restaurant is in the Norway castle. It reminded me of breakfasts I had in Germany. I loved it. The pastries were extremely delicious. And I don't mind having meat and cheese for breakfast.

Then the princesses greet you at your table. We had Kenzie ask them each a question so they would stay around longer at our table. They were all so friendly and fun to talk to. It was also great that Kenzie and Laney wore Cinderella and Belle dresses and those two princesses were there to take pictures with them. It was a dream come true for both girls because they both love princesses.

They also did a princess parade where they walked around the restaurant with the princesses.
