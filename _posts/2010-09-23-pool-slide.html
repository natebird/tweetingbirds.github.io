It was a hot summer day. The girls were bored and getting on each other's nerves while I was trying to get stuff done… craft projects for Kenzie's birthday party. So I set up the turtle pool and attached the slide. The turtle is supposed to be for a sandbox but I refuse to buy sand. And I don't want it to be the neighborhood kitty litter.

They had a wonderful time getting wet. Then I gave them yogurt popsicles from Japan. Yum! Laney's brain freeze face is the best.

Then when I went inside to grab towels they decided to get muddy. I tried to keep the pool clean, but it was pointless. They were having too much fun dumping dirt in the water.

At the end of the day I asked Kenzie if they had fun playing in the pool. This was her response…

tiffany: Kenzie, did you have fun playing in the pool outside?

Kenzie: It is was fun, especially when we stopped listening to you.

(They were dumping handfuls of dirt into the water.)
