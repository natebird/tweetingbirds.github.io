Wednesday night of last week, Kenzie and I went over to Adam and Amy's new house in Saratoga Springs. Their new house is a bit more wheelchair accessible for Sebastian's wheelchair. His bedroom is on the main floor and they have a cement wheelchair ramp for him at the front entrance of their house. Kenzie and I had so much fun visiting with the Hobsons and their three kids, Chloe, Victoria, and Sebastian. They are so big!

Kenzie had a great time playing with the girls' doll houses, Elmo dolls (which Victoria so nicely gave Kenzie one), and Sebastian's sensory learning toys. Amy made delicious dinner and Adam grilled chicken on his awesome gas grill. Both Chloe and Victoria were hilarious telling knock-knock jokes and all their Disney stories. They were at Disneyland the previous week. We ended the night with me playing some Guitar Hero for the first time on the Wii.
