This year we had quite the crowd for the annual gingerbread house contest and it was so fun. We had my grandma from Taiwan and my brother in town for Christmas. And we also invited our friend, Irene. I made potato soup in sourdough bread bowls and salad for dinner. Simple and fun. The kids loved it and so did the adults. Perfect too for cold winters as it was that week. Lai Lai didn't participate this year since she is still small. She just ate some graham crackers and candy. This year there were definitely some notable winners. My 82-year-old grandma had never done this before and she did pretty awesome.
