We finally made it home after 30 hours of traveling on a bus for 6 hours, two airplanes totaling 19 hours, a taxi, my Grandpa Boice's car, and my dad's car. Needless to say, we walked around like zombies today. We left Tainan at 1:40 p.m. (Taiwan time) on Sunday and walked in our front door Monday morning at 9 o'clock (Georgia time- there is a 12 hour difference in the time change).

To sum up our Taiwan trip, we had an amazing time. Our packed schedule kept us going all day long, and poor Kenzie didn't get much napping in during the whole trip. When she was tired enough, she fell asleep on our shoulders. I will detail the trip and post a lot of pictures later. This is just a quick number summary of our trip for trivia.
