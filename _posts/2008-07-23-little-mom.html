Kenzie loves to pretend she is Little Mom. It is really creepy having a mini-me following me around the house. She'll be the perfect big sister, though. She tells me, "Hold on, hold on, hold on," or "I'll be right back!" or "Just a minute!" or "Come on!" or "Dinner time!" or "Time to eat!" All phrases I guess I use too often around her. Her second birthday is coming up in a couple weeks! I've been holding off, but I guess we'll hit the potty training soon. Probably before Nate starts school so he can help me with it.

Other motherly things Kenzie does:

Carries as many things as she possibly can.

Spends hours in her kitchen cooking up a storm and feeding mommy, daddy, and all her animals.
Her menu consists of smoothies, candy, chicken nuggets, mac & cheese, or soup. Then she gives all the animals a bubble bath in the ball pit.
Puts her stuffed animals down for naps.

One day after her nap I found her playing with the diaper cream, getting it in the carpet, on her blanket, all over her clothes, in her hair, on her face, and inside that diaper on the floor.
She also loves to play with her farm animal, she calls it Grandpa's Farm from a song off Barney.
