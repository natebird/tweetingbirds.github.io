If you like, you can sign up to get email subscriptions of Tweeting Birds. Every time there is a new post on here, you will get the post in your email. That way you don't have to sign into here every time. To sign up for email subscription, you will need to visit the home page and on the sidebar on the right hand side you will see where you can enter your email address for email subscriptions.

And there is a button there to subscribe to this blog through your RSS reader. Even though this is a private blog, you can still read it in RSS reader or your email so you don't have to keep coming back here to check it.

Although, I don't mind you coming to the site often. I love for you to comment!

Just trying to make your life easier.
