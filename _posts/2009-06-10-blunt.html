Tiffany: Stop, Kenzie. Don't do that.
Kenzie: [with an apologetic face] Sorry, it's all my fault.
Tiffany: [chuckling a bit, giving Kenzie a kiss on her forhead] It's OK.
Kenzie: I don't want your kisses.
Tiffany: You don't want my kisses?!
Kenzie: You have food in your mouth, and it stinks.
