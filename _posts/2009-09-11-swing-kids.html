I'm going to forego the Flashback Friday today to catch up on our Labor Day weekend events. In addition to painting, decorating, going to the children's workshop, and visiting the botanical gardens, we also attend my family's monthly extended Family Home Evening on Sunday. AND we dressed in our college football shirts to get free Chic-fil-A sandwich on Labor Day. Thinking eating lunch at 2 p.m. the place wouldn't be too crowded, we were totally wrong. Chic-fil-A was jammed packed with people getting their free sandwich. We ordered to go and ate the food at a nearby park.

Of course I had to get pictures. It was a beautiful day and it was Laney's first time on a swing. I love that fall weather has been cooling off our temperatures around here.
