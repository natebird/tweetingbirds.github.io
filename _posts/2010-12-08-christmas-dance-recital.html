The dance school that Kenzie attends had a Christmas dance recital the weekend before Thanksgiving. It was nice for them to do this performance before their big Spring recital to prepare them to dance in front of a large crowd. Kenzie's class did a ballet routine to the song, "Do You See What I See." Kenzie did well. But she did forget some parts because she was watching the dancers. Kenzie is really good with her leg work, she's awesome with it. But she really needs to work on controlling her hands while doing the leg work. But the dance is really helping Kenzie with her coordination. And she really loves it.
