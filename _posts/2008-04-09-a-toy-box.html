Our house has been in need of a toy box for a while now. I was tired of seeing Kenzie's toys scattered in every room of the house. So I finally commissioned my grandma to make Kenzie a toy box. I have always wanted some furniture made by Grandpa, and now I finally have a toy box! It came unpainted so I can use the leftover black paint from the entertainment center project a month ago. I was eager to get the toy box finished and working in our house. So I painted three coats in two days, starting on Monday when my mom dropped off the toy box at our house. I let it baked outside in sunny 74 degree temperature. And had it polished off and in use by tonight. Here is the finished product in the toy room. Thanks, Grandpa!
