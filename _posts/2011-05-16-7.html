Happy anniversary to us! Yesterday was our anniversary hitting our 7 year mark. Couldn't have asked for a better husband to be by my side through this journey. I have been writing down our dating stories for Flashback Fridays and it is incredible how we got together. Here's us at the temple where we started our life together forever. I love this guy. Looking for many more years together. Like eternity.

  • I love that Nate is a hard worker and does so much to provide for our family. He is happiest when all of us are happy. He gives of himself so selflessly so his girls and wife can be happy. And he never cares about receiving credit or recognition for what he does for us or anyone.
  • I love that Nate is so willing to serve. He loves to help others and serve whenever he can. And he is often asked to do things through the church or family and he does so without a second thought.
  • I love that Nate helps out around the house with cleaning and cooking. He doesn't do the cooking as often as the cleaning. But he cooked meals whenever I'm am nauseated with pregnancy (which usually lasts about 4 months for me) and every once in a while Â when I'm tired of cooking.

  • I love that Nate is the calm one but takes charge when he needs to.
  • I love that Nate listens to me. I love talking to him about everything.
