Having 6 days of park passes, we spent 3 days at Magic Kingdom and one each day at each of the other parks. Loved them all. There's a lot to at each one of them. So OK, we were there at Disney World for a week and took over 1,100 pictures. So if you haven't noticed, there are a lot of pictures from our trip. Hope you can suffer through them all.

Hollywood Studios: Great for shows and the Toy Story Midway Mania ride.

In the building where we met Woody and Buzz they made waiting fun with photo ops.

We ate lunch at Pizza Planet. The pizzas were actually pretty good.

We watched the Beauty and the Beast show. It was awesome! Just look at Kenzie's reaction.

Played a little in the Honey, I Shrunk the Kids playground.

We also watched the Indiana Jones stunt show. Laney got scared a bit by all the explosion and such.

And the parade. Woody and Jessie.

Epcot: Nate and I would have loved this place if we didn't have the kids with us. They didn't care as much about Epcot as the adults. We did do some fun stuff and eat some awesome food from around the world. It was also awesome that we were there during the food and wine festival. That was also cool.

Japan. I even got to eat sushi there.

Garden of Beauty and the Beast characters.

Animal Kingdom: Nate loved this place. And they sold the best pretzel I've ever had… jalapeño cheese stuffed pretzel. I would totally go back to Disney just for that pretzel.

Safari ride. Laney loved seeing all the animals.

Lion King was the best show at Disney. I watched it twice.

Nemo play. That was actually cool how they did the play with the characters.
