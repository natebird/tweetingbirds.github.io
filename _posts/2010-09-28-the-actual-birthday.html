Kenzie's birthday was actually on a Monday. Her party wasn't until the Saturday following her birthday. So we did a little something for her on the day of her birthday. First I took her on a photo shoot. Then that afternoon we took her to watch Toy Story 3. I had vouchers for 3 movie tickets to watch Toy Story 3 (from cereal boxes and purchasing Toy Story 1 & 2). The great thing about going to watch the movie months after it released is you get the movie theater to yourself. Plus it was a Monday at 4 p.m. showing. There was one other couple in the theater with us. So I wasn't too worried if our kids made some noise. Laney did surprisingly well in the theater. And I also packed them snacks. That helped.

Then we had my parents come over for dinner and gifts. We gave Kenzie her gifts that night. And kept it low key. Saving up all our energy for her party on Saturday. Really? I have a 4-year-old?
