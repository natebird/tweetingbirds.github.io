*** Tender & Guilty Mommy Moment***

Yesterday, while I was upstairs getting some YW stuff done on the computer, Kenzie was downstairs playing. She came to the stairs and said, "Mommy, I very miss you."

Awww… I thought how sweet that she missed me while I'm just upstairs. Then I felt a bit guilty that I haven't spent much time with her because I was trying to get things done for church and around the house. "I miss you, too, Kenzie," I said.

"I miss you when you're at church at your meetings," Kenzie said to clarify. Oh, now I really felt guilty. She already knows that Daddy is gone all the time with school and Mommy is gone all the time with church meetings.

I don't know how other moms do it. Balance everything they need to do without feeling guilty that they are neglecting their children. I'm sure all moms feel guilty at some point. Maybe the question should be, how do moms balance everything and still make time for their kids and husband?
