You hear about all sorts of weird, made up holidays dedicated to celebrate something someone felt important. Well, now I have my own. Kenzie made it up for me.

As I am coming to the end of my 5th month of pregnancy, I am still frequenting the bathroom. At the sonogram, we found out it's because the little angel in me is breeched and kicking directly at my bladder.

In one of my many trips to the bathroom today, Kenzie joined me in the room and announced, "Mommy, it's your pee pee day in the bathroom. Now flush it."
