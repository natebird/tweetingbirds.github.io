"Heavenly Father,

Bless the sun and moon.
Bless our house.
Thank you for swimming.
Bless the movies.
Bless Curious George.
Jesus Christ,
Amen!"
Kenzie said that for our family prayer tonight. I broke down and laughed out loud when she said Curious George.
