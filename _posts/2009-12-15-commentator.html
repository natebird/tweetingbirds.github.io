Kenzie came home from nursery with a little booklet she made about thinking of Jesus during the sacrament.

Tiffany: [reading aloud to Kenzie] I can listen while the sacrament prayers are said.
Kenzie: Then the man child comes to bring us the sacrament.
Tiffany: [reading aloud] I can think of Jesus when I eat the bread.
Kenzie: I like eating the bread.
Tiffany: [reading aloud] I can think of Jesus when I drink the water.
Kenzie: And sometimes it drips on my dress.
Tiffany: [reading aloud] The sacrament helps me think about Jesus.
Kenzie: Yes it does.
