This year's fall fun we did as a family was go pick apples. We had been a couple years ago and enjoyed it. We thought we would go again this year. I got a Groupon deal where the girls also enjoyed pony rides, petting zoo, hayride, picking apples, and apple canon shoot. The girls had so much fun. We went on a Saturday morning before the crowd got bad. We left as everyone else were arriving and lines were getting long.

The girls loved feeding the goats. It was Lai Lai and Laney's first time riding a pony, Kenzie's second time. And when we did the canon shoot, Laney hit the target car that won her a free apple pie. She was so excited about that. We packed a picnic lunch and ate up there. Then came home with Southern goodies, fried fruit pies, apple cider, and boiled peanuts. I am on the only one in the family who liked boiled peanuts. And the pies were so delicious.
