My first time ever making a homemade pie completely from scratch. Yes, crust and all. It wasn't as arduous as I had made it out to be in my mind. However, it was time consuming. Mostly the crust part. But it was the best piece of peach pie I have ever tasted. I can totally see why people make homemade pie rather than the frozen ones from the grocery store. I used peaches we purchased from the farmer's market They kids enjoyed it too.
