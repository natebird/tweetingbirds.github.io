Our little Laney Lane is one of a kind. I love this girl to pieces. I hope she never changes because she is the spunk in our lives. Never a dull moment with this girl. You can't look at these pictures and not think she is the cutest ever. 
Some things about her right now. She loves olives. Green or black. Loves them and will stuff as many as she can into her mouth all at once. I gave her a little trim and now her bangs are straight. I trimmed a little on the back too. So the mullet on the back is even too. I'm trying to grow out her hair. Hate the mullet stage. The word 'No' doesn't mean anything to her. She'll do it unless you physically remove her from whatever she is not supposed to get into. 
Sh likes to get into EVERYTHING. There is not a spot in our house she hasn't explored yet. Every nook and cranny. She is the biggest reason why we can't find things in the house. And also the main reason why we can't keep the house clean. And the main reason we find things in random places. 

Still wants mom the hold her. Not as often as a month ago. And it mostly happens when I'm trying to make meals in the kitchen. Look at that pitiful face. 

Loves to try on shoes. Anyone's shoes. Even Nate's huge size 12 shoes. She has a thing about shoes, especially a pair of Kenzie's pink mary jane shoes. She will wear those around the house all the time. And when she wants to go outside, she'll bring you her shoes. But when she is sitting in her car seat in the car, she loves to take her shoes off. 

Makes the funniest faces.

She is an outdoorsy kind of girl. Loves to be outside. Will play in dirt, grass, sand, mud, maybe even eat some of it. When she can't be outside, she will stare out the window dreaming about playing outside. 

Loves pushing around the shopping cart (actual store carts and toy ones) and doll stroller. Will push them even if they are flipped upside down. She also enjoys crashing them.

Wants to sit at the table like everyone else. Occasionally we put her at the table with us sitting on a step stool. But she can't be trusted on that thing. Will jump off without a thought. And she also loves to drink out of everyone else's cup except her own. 
Learning to eat like a big girl. Also loves to make a huge mess. Will drop food off her high chair tray only to come back later to eat it off the floor. 

Loves to hang out in the kitchen with me and help me cook. Will always help stir, mix, pour and taste anything. 

Will not be restrained by a high chair. She will get up and either sit on the tray part to get food all over her bums or put her foot on the tray to push food off it. Occasionally she'll do both. She also will attempt to jump out of there when she is done. Or sit completely on the tray part. Or sit back down on the food that has gotten on the seat. Whatever it is she is doing it sure makes a big mess each meal time.
