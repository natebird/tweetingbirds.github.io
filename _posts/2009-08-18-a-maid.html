At the dining room table.

Kenzie: Mom, I need to find a clean chair.
Tiffany: You chair is clean. I cleaned it after lunch, remember?
Kenzie: [looking at her chair] Oh, yeah. But the table is dirty.
Tiffany: Who made the mess?
Kenzie: I did.
Tiffany: Then who should clean it up?
Kenzie: [answers a bit too quickly] You, mom.
Tiffany: What?!
Kenzie: I mean us. You and me.
