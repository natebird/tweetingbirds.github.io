Kenzie has been such a loving and nice big sister. I don't really have to worry about her hurting Laney. She loves to give Laney hugs and kisses. When Laney is crying, Kenzie tells her that it'll be OK and mommy is coming. Then Kenzie comes to me and tells me that Laney is hungry and needs mommy's milk. Although, the other night when Laney started crying Kenzie told her, "There's no whining in this house."

Since Kenzie had been sick with a cold, I have been doing my best to keep the two away from each other. There are times when Kenzie really wants to hold or pat Laney. I keep telling Kenzie that she has to stay away from Laney because she is sick. Kenzie responded with, "But I drank some juice and I'm all better now." Or she said, "I just need to be gentle with her." It was hard keeping Kenzie away when she just wanted to help her little sister.
