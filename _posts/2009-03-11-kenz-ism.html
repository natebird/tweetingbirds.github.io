[Sliding down the stairs.] "Slide starts with a 'M'. Mmmm-slide."

Kenzie: I want to go to school with Dad, but I'm too little big.
Tiffany: You can go to school when you are 4. [Holding up four fingers.] How old are you now?
Kenzie: 2 and half [Holding up two fingers on one hand and one on the other hand.]
Tiffany: You are more than half way there. But I'll miss you when you go to school because Mommy can't go with you. You have to go all by yourself.
Kenzie: You will miss me?
Tiffany: Yup. But we'll have fun together when you get home from school.
Kenzie: I have an idea! You can decorate the house and surprise me when I get home from school.
