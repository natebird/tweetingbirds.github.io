Non stop taking coming out of such a little girl. It's fun to see what she's thinking about, what's cranking in that little brain of hers.

  • I did it! - when she does something by herself.
  • Can I have it? - when she sees something she wants
  • Come on. - when she wants us to follow her. She usually grabs your finger and says this.
  • Just a minute.
  • Can I have some? - when she wants us to share with her.
  • Be careful.
  • Be yourself. - she picked up the phrase from her show, Super Why.
  • Save water. - also a phrase from Super Why.
  • Hugs and kisses time. - a family tradition after family prayers at night.
  • I'll get it.
  • Daddy's home! - she loves it when she hears the garage door open. She usually greets him at the door.
  • I go downstairs.
  • All right.
  • Thank you, welcome.
  • Strawberry yogurt. - her favorite.
  • [Item or person] falls down.
  • Mommy help.
  • Hands dirty. Wash hands. - she says this several times a day. I'm afraid some of my OCD tendencies has rubbed off on her.

  • Puo Puo shopping.
