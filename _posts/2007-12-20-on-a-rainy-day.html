Yesterday afternoon we finally got some much needed rain. It's been cold and dreary here the past few days. I don't mind the rain, but my body is not made for the cold weather (meaning highs in the mid 50 degrees here). I was checking my email in our office upstairs when I heard Kenzie blabbering on and on to herself. I decided grab a sneak peek at what she was doing in the guest room (where I heard her talk). Here she was reading aloud to herself, snuggling up to her favorite blanket and sitting in her old car seat. What a cutie... just like her mom. I, too, enjoy snuggling up to a good book on a cold and rainy day.
