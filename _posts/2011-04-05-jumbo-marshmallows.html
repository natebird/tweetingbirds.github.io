My parents bought these campfire jumbo marshmallows for the girls for Christmas. Thanks, mom and dad. You only need one marshmallow to cover one graham cracker to make s'mores. Oh but how our children love them. I only let them roast one and eat that. It's ridiculous how much sugar is in one of those!
