This year I decided to go with an ice cream theme. It's August. It's hot. And Kenzie loves ice cream. Can't go wrong, right? So I started coming up with the ideas and making all the stuff for the party a month ahead. Kenzie's only request was a piñata. So off I went to try to make one. If you follow my website, Simply Modern Mom, you probably saw all the tutorials I posted for these things I made for the party. Go here to see all the tutorials and party details.

I made ice cream invitations and calligraphied the info on there. I also made the envelopes using paper lunch sacks. I used bakers twine in a lot of the tutorials. I love using bakers twine. I ordered mine from Divine Twine. They have fabulous colors.

For the party favor that I sent everyone home with was astronaut ice cream. I wrapped it nicely and it was take home ice cream.

I also made the goodie bags for the kids to put candy from the piñata.
