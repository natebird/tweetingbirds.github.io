We have been going to pumpkin patches for the last 3 years. It was never meant to be this way, but we have visited a different one each year. This one we went to Stone Mountain because we have a membership there. We love Stone Mountain. So much to do there. The girls love it too. We got the pass in July and we have already been there about five times. We still haven't seen and done everything there.

Anyway, we went there for the pumpkin patch and it was the perfect place to snap a few photos of the girls. It was a gorgeous fall day. They also had a bunch of outside games for the girls to participate. We went on a scavenger hunt. We decorated a mini pumpkin. We fished for rubber duckies. We then went on the Duck Boat (a vehicle that drive on the road and floats in the water).
