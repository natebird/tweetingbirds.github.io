When Nate got home from school Thursday night, there were barely enough snow to make a little snowman. The snow kept coming down through the night. The next morning we have a beautiful view of sunshine and white coverings. But the snowman looked like he got mugged and shot by a bigger snowman. And it has been cold enough here that our snowman was still standing a week later.
