I come down to the kitchen. First thing I see…

Then I saw this…

Mind you, this was around 11 a.m. I asked…

Tiffany: Kenzie, did you eat all 3 cupcakes?
Kenzie: I sure did. I had an early snack.
Tiffany: First of all, we don't eat cupcakes this early. Second, you're supposed to ask first before you eat snacks.
Kenzie: [not remorseful at all] Sorry, mom.

Later, Nate came down for lunch to seeing the same thing.

Nate: Kenzie, did you eat all the cupcakes?
Kenzie: Yes. For my snack. I was supposed to ask first. But I'm still happy.
