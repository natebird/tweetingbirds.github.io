I don't know what happened and one day this adorable girl is 1. Her birthday comes at a hard time. Right after school starting and Kenzie's birthday. For her birthday we just had a little party for her at home. My parents came over along with Jeffrey. We had cupcakes and ice cream and opened presents. Nate wanted to get out the ball pit for Lai Lai's birthday. She wasn't fond of it at first but eventually warmed up to it. We got her a Build-A-Bear just like her sisters. She would steal her sister's bears and run away. So we thought we would get her one.

In a way, she has always seem older to me because she started walking and doing things at such a young age.
