Our dear friends, Richard and Tannen, moved yesterday. Sad day. We had so much in common. We are pretty much living their life, just a year behind.
  • Richard and Tannen met at BYU. Nate and I met at BYU. That's pretty common. Millions of couples met at BYU.
  • Tannen comes from a large family of nine kids. Nate comes from a large family of 8 kids.
  • Tannen is from Oregon. Nate is from Washington. Both from the west coast.
  • Richard and I both grew up and have family in Georgia.
  • Richard and Nate both majored in economics at BYU.
  • Richard and Tannen have a daughter, Hailey, who has the same birthday as Kenzie - just a year older.
  • Richard and Nate both attended the same college for their MBAs - Nate is finishing a year after.
  • Richard and Tannen have a second daughter, Julia, who doesn't have the exact birthday as Laney, but she is a year older.
Tannen and I are similar in so many ways it's actually funny, at least to our husbands who laugh at us. So many wonderful memories with them over the past three years.
We're going to miss them. Kenzie has been saying, "Hailey moved far, far away. But maybe she can come back and we can play again."
