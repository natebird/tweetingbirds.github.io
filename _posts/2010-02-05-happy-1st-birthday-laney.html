We had her first birthday party on Friday night. It was a Chinese theme. We all dressed in our Chinese clothes. Put up some paper lanterns with her photos from her photo shoot dangling below it. Served fried rice, fried chicken with sweet and spicy sauce and my mom's awesome egg rolls. Her birthday cake was a carrot cake made from scratch. I also made cupcakes for her to dig into. She had a great time and was a good sport for not having her second nap for the day.
