Rather than getting into nesting like a good mom, I have been procrastinating. I have made improvements though! Yesterday I got a baby monitor for the new one. I had Nate get baby stuff down from the attic and a small luggage for my hospital bag. Every once in a while, when I see something I will need, I throw it in the luggage. I'm not quite sure what I have in there and what I still need to put in there.

A quick OB update (skip this of you're sensitive to words such as membrane or dilated): At my OB appointment yesterday the midwife said I'm dilated to 2 cm (a little slower than I was with Kenzie). At my next appointment, coming up Friday, they will strip my membrane to see if that does anything. And they will also set up a date for me to be induced. I am not allowed to go over my due date since I have gestational diabetes.

I am hoping I can have the baby on the 29th so we can be home by Sunday. And then Nate won't have to miss his class on Monday. Hey, he's paying $130 per class, so if he doesn't have to miss a class, that would be great.

Until then, I'm holding the baby in, hoping she doesn't come early. I have too much to get done and I'm not sure if two weeks will be enough time. It hasn't been too uncomfortable, except for sleeping. So I don't mind going to my due date.
