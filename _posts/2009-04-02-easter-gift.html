Now that I have the Pocahontas toy in hand, I have been trying to convince Kenzie that perhaps Pocahontas will arrive for Easter rather than her birthday in August. I have been having a conversation about how Pocahontas is missing several times a day with this girl. Yesterday, one of the conversations went as follows.

Kenzie: Mom, my Pocahontas toy is missing. The store took my Pocahontas toy.
Tiffany: You don't have a Pocahontas toy.
Kenzie: Maybe I'll get her for my birthday.
Tiffany: What if she comes for Easter?
Kenzie: No, she is coming for my birthday.
Tiffany: But Easter is next Sunday. Your birthday is not until August, that's a long time from now.
Kenzie: No, Pocahontas has to come to my birthday party.

Dang. But today she may be changing her mind.

Kenzie: Mom, I'm missing my Pocahontas toy.
Tiffany: Where is she?
Kenzie: She's at the store. [Throwing both hands up and out to the sides as in a-matter-of-fact.]
Tiffany: Is she going to come visit you?
Kenzie: Yeah, maybe for my Easter birthday.
