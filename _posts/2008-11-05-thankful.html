I am thankful for my doctor. He help fix me.
I am thankful for the kitchen.
I am thankful for the TV.
I am thankful for my table (in her playroom).
I am thankful for yellow.
I am thankful for thunder.
I am thankful for paper.
I am thankful for crayons.

Then there were the usual: Mommy, Daddy, Puo Puo, Dudepa, Jeffrey, GG, Great Grandpa, Grandma, Grandpa, Elmo, Baby Bop, Barney, and toys.
