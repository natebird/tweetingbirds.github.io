The night before Easter Nate and I set up a princess hunt for Kenzie. We gathered all her princesses, including Pocahontas, and hid them in different places downstairs. I drew pictures of where each princess was hiding and numbered them with Pocahontas being last.

Easter morning, before Laney's freak out, we told Kenzie that her princesses are playing hide and go seek. They left her clues and she had to go find them. She got totally into it and loved every moment. The Easter bunny also brought her Sleeping Beauty movie and a princess magic wand book. I got both gifts for less than $4 each, what a deal.

Laney watched the whole thing in her new toy, ExerSaucer. Look at how well she is holding her head up.

The next day, Kenzie wanted to hide all the princesses again and use the clues to find them. She even remember which princess hid where.
