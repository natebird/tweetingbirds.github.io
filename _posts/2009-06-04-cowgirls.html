I got side tracked from making more shirt dresses when I found bandanas at the thrift store for 50 cents each. I also found a huge white bed sheet to use as the trims on these skirts. I'm usually not big into the western cowboy styles, but I thought they turned out pretty cute. Super simple to make and took no time at all if I could just get it done in one sitting.
