Another week without a Flashback Friday and I just noticed right now, Sunday at 9:04 p.m. Where have my mind been? I don't know. I am at that stage of pregnancy where I am overly spacey and clumsy. I am dropping things left and right several times.

Today, on our way out the door to visit Scott and Stacey (uncle and auntie), I asked Nate as we were getting in the car…

Tiffany: Did you get the house key?
Nate: Yes, you gave it to me just a few minutes ago.
Tiffany: Did I really? Because I do remember thinking I should probably get the keys but I don't remember actually giving it to you.
Nate: I remember watching you handing me the keys.
Tiffany: Oh.

And this week, Nate and I haven't seen much of each other at all, even less than usual.

Monday: Nate had school and stayed after to meet with his group for a project.
Tuesday: I had an OB appointment then ran errands. Nate had school that evening and came home late again for another group project.
Wednesday: I had a presidency meeting with YW. I didn't have mutual that evening, but Nate went to the church to play basketball.
Thursday: I had my glucose test for 3 hours. I ran errands. Nate had school in the evening. And he had a conference call with classmates when he got home. Some of the sisters in the ward came over to do a run through of the tile boards for the holiday workshop.
Friday: I ran more errands all day. Nate said we couldn't do anything but stay home and watch a movie this evening. It was our first family night in a long time.
Saturday: I was at the church from 7:30 a.m. to 4 p.m. with the Relief Society Holiday Workshop. I came home and was just exhausted from standing up that whole time. My parents came over for dinner and hung out in the evening.
Sunday: I was at the church from 7:20 a.m. to 1:45 p.m. for church and three meetings. Then we went over to Scott and Stacey's house.

Oh, how I miss Nate!
