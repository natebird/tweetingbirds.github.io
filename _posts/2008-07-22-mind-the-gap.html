Warning: this is extremely long!

I am so upset and I am determined to get some pictures before the end of summer. Kenzie loves the pool and "swimming" in the water with her floaties. I even shoved her down the pool's slide all by herself and she survived - sitting up at first, then laying down, turning, and eventually coming out at the end face down and head first, she didn't drink any water though.

Family: My parents finally came home after their six week long vacation to Taiwan and Thailand. It has been nice having them back, but family events has popped up frequently since they have been back. Kenzie missed her Dudepa and Puo Puo. The day Nick left, my step-sister, Claire, and her son, Jayden, came into town from Boston for a family visit. We had a big family get together last Wednesday night for her. It was nice to visit with Claire since the last time I saw her was six years ago.

Since then, we have been trying to order the one part we need to fix it. We should be able to pick it up from the dealer this week and have it fixed before more rain heads our way.

We are also in need of a second car. Dread. With Nate going back to school and working full-time, a second child coming, my OB appointments, and our church callings (Nate in Elder's quorum presidency and I'm in YW presidency), our child(ren)'s wellness checkups, and grocery shopping, it's time for a #2 car. Once again, no pictures because we thought we forgot our camera when it was actually sitting in the diaper bag. But we did get a video of Nate throwing Kenzie into the sky in the pool.
