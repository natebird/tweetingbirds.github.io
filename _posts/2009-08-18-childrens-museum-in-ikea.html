Last week we attempted to go to the Children's Museum. It was a free day and it was over crowded. When we arrived, angry mothers were leaving the parking lot complaining about the line wrapping around the building. I thought that with the kids back in school, there wouldn't be such a huge crowd. But the museum actually had to turn people away due to maximum capacity. So we resorted to the next best thing… Ikea. We were with some friends who hadn't been to Ikea. We took the kiddos there to play in their children's section. Great times.
