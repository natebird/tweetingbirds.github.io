This General Conference we went over to my parents' house with our family friends, Irene and Barron. We had a TON of food - during conference snacks and between conference potluck and second session snacks. We didn't go hungry for a minute. It was a gorgeous day outside at 80 degrees so we had lunch outside in the courtyard.

Kenzie and I made prophet and apostle cards so she can learn about those who were speaking.

My mom was great at keeping the girls entertain by playing beauty shop with them and then they put on a fashion show for us. The girls loved it. They both love playing dress up.
