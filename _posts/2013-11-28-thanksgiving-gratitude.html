We have so much to be grateful for this year. Blessed beyond our means! Recently in a Relief Society activity we were challenged to list 100 things we are grateful for and they had categories. Here's my list…

10 Living People You are Grateful For

  1. Nate. He is my best friend. I am always amazed at our relationship and how well we get along all the time. I love being married to him. He is absolutely perfect for me. We know exactly when to be there for each other and support each other when the other one is down. We hardly ever argue. We have had disagreements but we never yelled at each other before. We both completely trust each other with everything and know that we are both doing our best with our marriage and family life. He has his strengths and I have mine and they work well complementing each other. I am so glad we found each other in college. It was destiny, fate, and the perfect love story.
  2. Kenzie. She is mini me from day one. She is my helper and my little buddy. She makes life easy by being so obedient. She loves school and does her homework on her own. Doesn't complain about having to practice piano. She does her best each day to choose the right and be an example to her sisters.
  3. Laney. She is the spunk in the family. I love her creative spirit and energy. She is curious about the world around her. She is also observant. She is a fighter and doesn't let things stop her from achieving her dreams. She is such a girly girl but not afraid to play in the dirt.
  4. Lai Lai. She is not a pushover, she knows how to defend herself. She is sweet and thoughtful of others. She is mommy's girl, and I love that. She can do what anyone else is doing, especially her sisters. She is so talkative. She doesn't like to be left out of things.
  5. One on the way. We are so excited for this next baby girl. She will fit right in with all the girls. We have been blessed with all our children. Can't wait to meet her.
  6. My mom. She is the most charitable and friendly person I know. She is always thinking of others and what she can do the help them. She is especially good at making friends with people from other countries, even if she can't communicate well with them due to language barriers.
  7. My dad. He has been a great dad to all of us. We have a lot of fun together and great conversations. He has always inspired me that it is never too late to learn something new. And to create your own adventures.
  8. Cyndi. I adore Nate's parents. Cyndi is such a great mom and I learn so much from her. She raised eight wonderful children.
  9. Randy. He is such a hard worker and a people person. Very humble. I love that Nate is his father's twin. They are great men.
  10. Jeffrey. My only brother. We have many great memories together. It has always been the two of us, and we take care of each other.

10 People Who Have Died You are Grateful For

  1. Agong (my grandpa). He helped raised me when I was little living in Taiwan. I remember him singing songs to me at bedtime and teaching me Japanese. We would watch Japanese sword fighting shows together. He would walk me to school and pick me up after school.
  2. Joseph Smith. Restoring the gospel so I can be a part of it.
  3. President Gordon B. Hinckley. He influenced me so much throughout me teen and college years. I love his relationship with his wife and his humor too.

10 Physical Abilities You are Grateful For

  1. My vision. Being able to see my children and watch them grow. Being able to take pictures. Being able to see sunsets and the beauty around me. I also enjoy reading.
  2. Cooking. Although I don't like to do it because the children always complain about the food, I am blessed to be able to cook for my family so we don't have to eat out all the time.
  3. Crafting. I love being able to use my mind and hands to create things for my family and our home.
  4. My hearing. I love to listen to my girls play nicely together and teach each other. I love hearing their laughs and giggles. I like listening to their stories and how their day went. I especially love hearing them sing and play the piano.
  5. Taste. I love food so I love being able to taste food.
  6. Exercise and dance. I'm not the best at exercising but I am grateful that I have the ability to get in shape and stay healthy and keep up with my kids.
  7. Energy to keep up with my children. They are everywhere and I am grateful that I can run around with them and play with them.
  8. Driving. We would be in a world of hurt if I wasn't able to drive my family around to where we need to go.
  9. Give hugs and snuggles. I love getting hugs and giving hugs. I love to snuggle with my kids and Nate. It's my favorite things.
  10. Walking. I think it's something we take for granted. It's one of those things you take for granted until you can't do it.

10 Material Possessions You are Grateful For

  1. My camera and lenses. With it I am able to capture precious moments of my family and preserve memories. It is so important to me to have photographs of my family and the fun things that we do together and to watch them grow.
  2. My scriptures. It's a roadmap of how to return to Heavenly Father. It brings me comfort when I am down. I have so much to learn from it.
  3. My wedding ring. Nate customized it just for me. I love it. And I love being married to him.
  4. My minivan. Although it's not as cool as a SUV, it comfortably fits all of us. It also helps us to haul stuff which we never had the ability to do so before with our little economy cars. And the biggest thing is that it gets us to all the various places we need to go, especially on some vacations.
  5. My computer. Allows me to work on writing, organize my photos, and communicate. And it give Nate a job.
  6. Shoes. I love shoes. I can't help it.
  7. Piano. I love listening to my children play and I would love to learn to play one day too.
  8. My bed. I love to get a good night sleep.
  9. Vitamix. We have been making great smoothies, soups, and milkshakes with it. It is one way for me to get vegetables into my children.
  10. Books and magazines. I love to read and look at pictures.

10 Things About Nature You are Grateful For

  1. Sunshine. I love the sunshine. It always makes me feel good and happy.
  2. Ocean. I grew up next to the ocean so I love the smell of it. Feeling the ocean breeze. Being able to play in the water and sand.
  3. Waterfalls. They are so gorgeous. We would hike to waterfalls in Hawaii all the time.
  4. Seeing the spring and fall colors. The colors are always gorgeous and remind me how blessed I am.
  5. Fruits and vegetables. Having fresh fruits is a wonderful thing. I especially love tropical fruits.
  6. Mountains. They are a gorgeous scenery.
  7. Georgia thunder storms. The energy from a good ol' Georgia thunderstorm is amazing.
  8. Sunsets and sunrises. I love the gorgeous colors it brings.
  9. Grass to play in. I miss the grass in Hawaii where we can sit and walk around in it without worrying about chiggers or bugs like the Georgia grass.
  10. Trees. I love tall trees. I love seeing the leaves change colors in the fall. I love pictures of trees. I love big trees to climb on. I used to play and crawl in a mango tree when I was a little girl.

10 Things About Today You are Grateful For

  1. Nate has job(s). We've been through unemployment and that was not fun. So everyday where he is able to bring home a paycheck is greatly appreciated.
  2. Great friends. We have been so blessed with good friends in our lives. And even my children have good friends at church and at school.
  3. Stay-at-home mom. I am extremely grateful that I am able to be home with the children. I am able to be involved in their lives, take them to activities, get to know their friends' parents, and have some time to myself.
  4. Quiet times. I need that break every day.
  5. The time we have with loved ones. We have such wonderful family and group of friends. We are so blessed with all the time we have with them.
  6. My education. I am grateful that I have a college degree. I love to share my talent to help others. I love to learn. I love that I am able to teach my children. I would love to get a Master's Degree one day when the kids are all in school.
  7. Lifestyle. Being able to provide my family the lifestyle that we like. We don't care for anything fancy but to be able to go on trips, have a comfortable home and the things that we do have. Being able to put the kids in dance and piano. And so many other stuff.
  8. My health. I am grateful for my health. The ability to birth four children. The fact that we are healthy for the most part.
  9. Current church calling. As the Public Affairs Media Specialist, I love that I get to do what I enjoy doing for my church calling. It is such a wonderful calling to share the amazing things we do as a church with those in our communities. It's such a perfect calling for me. I hope I keep this calling for a long time.
  10. Amazing teachers. My girls have such great teachers at school, piano, and dance who are so wonderful in working with the girls.

10 Places on Earth You are Grateful For

  1. Our home. Wherever it may be, I love to make it a place that we create memories and are comfortable.
  2. My parents' house. It's a place for memory and comfort.
  3. Nate's parents' house. Such a gorgeous place. And Nate's dad built it himself.
  4. Hawaii. It was such a great place to grow up!
  5. Temples. Where else do you go to gain eternal perspective and feel the Savior's love? And it's the peace that comes with going.
  6. Sundance, UT. It was my place to get away from it all during college and enjoy nature.
  7. BYU. Great education and the place where I met my husband and some of my best friends.
  8. Italy. I want to go there some day.
  9. Disney World. We really do have fun together as a family there. And everyone is happy.
  10. USA. The freedom which we so often take granted of. The right to vote. The freedom of religion and speech. And what a beautiful country it is too.

10 Modern Day Inventions You are Grateful For

  1. Electricity. I like being able to see at night by turning on the light. Do my laundry. Straighten my hair. Have heat and AC.
  2. Plumbing. Indoor plumbing is a wonderful thing. And being able to shower and have clean dishes and clothes.
  3. Airplanes. So we can travel. We love traveling!
  4. Video chats. We have lots of family and friends who live far away from us. It is nice to be able to keep in touch and communicate with them.
  5. Internet. Makes the world a smaller place.
  6. Amazon.com. It's great to be able to shop online, find great prices and have it shipped to our house for free.
  7. GPS. I have a horrible sense of direction. This helps me get around.
  8. Hot showers. Grateful for that water heat pump that allows me to take hot showers. It's so relaxing.
  9. Smartphones. It is helpful in helping me keep in touch with friends and family. Grateful that long distance phone calls are now the same price as any other phone calls. And grateful for it's photo taking abilities so I can have even more pictures!
  10. Modern medicine. It has helped so many lives around me, including myself and my own children.

10 Foods You are Grateful For

  1. Persimmons. My absolute favorite fruit.
  2. Seafood. I love just about all seafoods. So delicious!
  3. Ice cream. We are rarely out of ice cream at our home.
  4. French macarons. They are delicate and so delicious, yet hard to find.
  5. Avocados. Super yummy in just about any way.
  6. Artichokes. I'll eat anything with artichokes in it.
  7. Arugula. My favorite thing to add to a salad.
  8. Cafe Rio salad. Need I say more?
  9. Freshly baked breads and pastries. I am a sucker for baked good.
  10. Soy milk. I rarely drink cow's milk. I love soy milk and steam it in the winter time. Reminds me of the steam milk I used to get up in Sundance during my college years.
