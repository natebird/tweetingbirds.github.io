One of Laney's friends in dance class, Addy, had her birthday party at the dance studio. I took just Laney to the party while Nate stayed home with Kenzie and they put up Christmas lights outside. Laney and I had such fun at the party. This was her first time going to a friend's birthday party by herself. And of course I forgot my camera. So this is all I have, pictures from my phone. Thank goodness for my phone camera.

For the party, their dance teacher had princess costumes for everyone. They got to choose which one they wanted. Then they did a few dance fundamentals, played with hula hoops, balls, the parachute, balance beam and lots of other fun stuff. It was a cute Strawberry Shortcake party. Laney had so much fun, she still talks about the party. She has such a good group of girls in her dance class.
