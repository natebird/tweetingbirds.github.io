  2. I made my own baby wrap that I am going to try to use with the new baby. I did lots of research on baby wraps and figured it would be cheaper for me to try to make my own. Actually, Kenzie and Laney gave the ties to Nate a little later. But Lai Lai and the new baby got their ties ahead of time. I printed a bunch of Instagram photos from Prinstagram and they turned out really great. I have been wanting to put together a collage of some of our every day life images on the wall. We had a plain wall in our living room. I love it.

I crocheted some bun covering for the girls' dance classes. And I glued rhinestones on them to make them look a little nicer. Maybe this will encourage me to put their hair up in a bun for class rather than just a ponytail. I also ordered more Whirl a Style to make buns faster and easier. Been working great!

I wanted to make some felt hair clips for the new baby and for the girls. We put up a ribbon board in their bathroom for all their nicer hair clips. I added to the board with these simple and fast felt bow hair clips.

I had ordered candy bracelets for Kenzie's class for Valentine's Day. I was trying to think of a card to make with it for her to hand out in class on Valentine's Day. This was what I came up with. I wanted to get Valentine's Day stuff prepared and ready to go just in case I have the baby early. Then I don't have to worry about it with a baby around.

And lastly, I did a makeover on our silhouette frames. I wanted something a little more simple but colorful. And I needed to update Laney's. I wanted to have each girls' silhouette of around age 4-5. That's when I made Kenzie's was when she was 4. I made one of Lai Lai now, but I'll replace it when she is 4 with an updated one. And I went ahead and made a spot for the new baby too. I'll put one in there of her when she gets older. When I purchased the frames a few years ago, I went ahead and bought 6 frames thinking we would have 4 kids. The extra frames had been sitting in a closet all these years. Now they are all up and I like it.
