This past Tuesday it rained here. Kenzie looked outside into our backyard and saw a nice puddle. She said, "Mom, I want to go splash in the water. I'll go get my shoes." Off she went to get her shoes and she came back with it on correctly. Then she said, "Come on, mom. Let's go splash in the water outside."

Here are pictures of all the fun she had splashing around in our backyard. And yes, once again, she is in her pajamas. They were already dirty from yogurt down the front. I figured the puddle would probably wash it out. When she got all her splashing done, we went back inside for a nice warm bath.

Loving it!
Look at the vertical on that girl!
