I got Kenzie this new set of wheels at the thrift store for $2. She can't quite reached the pedals yet, but she scoots around Fred Flintstone style. She can reach her metal tricycle's pedals, but she likes this one better. I'm trying to get her to switch over and teach her to pedal.

More chalk fun outside yesterday because it was so nice with the sunshine. Kenzie says it's "Daddy, Mommy, Kenzie, and churp churp (bird)."

She loves being outside in the sunshine. It's great to have 70 degrees weather so we can enjoy the sunshine.

She can make a mess even outside! We played with bubbles for a while then moved on to chalks. And now it's all over the place. She did help pick it all up, though.

She loves to sit on that ledge for some reason. I guess it's because it's her size.
