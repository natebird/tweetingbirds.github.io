I started my own fashion line of crochet flower hair clips. Here are the first three styles, using Laney as my model. She's good at giving the serious look. I have many more styles coming up with leaves, other cute colors, and other style flowers.

I haven't decided on what to do with these hair clips, whether I want to sell it to the public or what. We can work something out or perhaps set up some sort of barter system. :)
