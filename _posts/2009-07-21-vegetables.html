She started eating vegetables a couple weeks ago. We started out with carrots, then squash, and now green beans. She loved them all.

I also forgot to post about her last wellness check. The nurse at the pediatrician's office was so impressed by Laney sitting up all on her own at 5 months. She even called other nurses to come in and see. She was 25 1/2" tall and weighed 17 1/2 lbs.

Laney is at such a fun age. She sits and plays. Not yet mobile. However, she has been trying to craw a bit. She wants to get in the middle of everything. Doesn't want to miss out on any fun that her sister may be having.

I finally got her down on a consistent routine of awake for 2 hours, nap for 2 hours, and sleeps through the night for 11-12 hours straight. She is such a trooper for when we are out and about. Sleep is a top priority in our house because I don't function well without a good 8-9 hours each night.
