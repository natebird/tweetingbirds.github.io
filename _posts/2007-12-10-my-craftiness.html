I made this wreath because I couldn't find a wreath that I liked. But I love this one that I made! That's what's great about making your own. You can make it the way you want it.
I made these stockings from our Enrichment Super Saturday. There are actually 6 designs but I only put up these three for each member of our family. They are blanket stitched fleece and felt stockings. The other three designs are candy cane, gingerbread men, and a pair of mittens.
I also made this family of mice for Kenzie to play at church as her quiet toys. There are actually six baby mice. They live in a block of cheese. My grandma made these same toys for her kids and other grandchildren. I got her patterns to make these.
I am now working on more quiet toys like the mice but a family of bears that lives in a cupcake, a farm family of bunnies that lives in a carrot, and a family of circus elephants that lives in a peanut.
