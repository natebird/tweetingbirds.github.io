Sometime Kenzie's excessive humming can be annoying and Nate was trying to find someone to blame for it.

Kenzie: [humming to her own made up tune] Hum… Hum… Hum…
Nate: Kenzie, where did you learn how to hum?
Kenzie: In my bed.
Nate: Who taught you how to hum? [attempting to get Kenzie to say Tiffany so he can blame her]
Kenzie: You did, Dad.
Nate in thought: Dang.

A moment later.

Kenzie: What are you singing, Dad?
Nate: Some song I heard on the radio. I don't even know the words.

It's a musical morning at our house today.
