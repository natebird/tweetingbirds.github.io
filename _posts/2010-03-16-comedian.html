Kenzie: Mom, I need to talk to you.
Tiffany: What do you need to talk to me about?
Kenzie: Mom, why do princesses like to play with ogres?
Tiffany: Why do princesses like to play with ogres?
Kenzie: Because they like to just swing it. [swinging her arms]
Tiffany: [laughing because I'm not quite sure what that's supposed to mean.]
Kenzie: How do ducks fly?
Tiffany: I don't know. How do ducks fly?
Kenzie: Because they like to swing it. [swinging her arms]

OK, so it wasn't really funny. But it was her first attempts at telling jokes. Got to give her credit.
