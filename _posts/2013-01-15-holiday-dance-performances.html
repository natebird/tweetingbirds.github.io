The girls had holiday dance performances where the parents got to go in the last 10 minutes of class to watch them perform a holiday number. Both girls are doing well in dance and are still enjoying it. Although at times I have to convince Kenzie to go to class. She says she would rather stay home and have play time rather than go to class. I have to remind her that even if she didn't do dance, she would be doing something else and not have play time every day of the week.

Kenzie's class performed "I Want a Hippopotamus for Christmas" in tap. Laney's class performed "Frosty the Snowman" in ballet and "Jingle Bells" in tap.
