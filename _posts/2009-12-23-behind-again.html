Giving it to Laney who was also teething. Then Kenzie had croup. Last week, Laney ended up with an ear infection where the pressure erupted her ear drum. Work
After over a year of searching and applying to jobs, Nate finally found one. It's a contract job doing programming for a start-up business in Utah. We are so glad that he had a friend who recommended Nate to the company. It really is all about networking. What is great about this job is the flexible hours. He can work as many hours as he wants and whatever hours that best fits his schedule. Such a blessing since Nate is still in school. On top of this job, Nate has also been contacted by a few others who want him to re-branding and re-design of their websites. To say the least, he is busy. But in a good way.
