I did a good job dropping the first 15 pounds before going to Disney World last year. But I still had the last 5 pounds I wanted to drop before I turned 30. That last 5 pounds were stubborn. I stayed there for a good 6 months. So after the new year, I decided to get serious again about losing weight. Although I didn't gain any weight over the holidays, I could feel my clothes fitting differently. Not in a nice way. Then in March I got a stomach bug for a few days. That helped me drop 2 pounds. So I had three more to go. I got back into the gym but could only make it twice a week at the most because of all the stuff going on. We've been really busy lately. I after our Utah trip, I was two pounds away. Still two more weeks to go. I'll see if I can make it.

Nate also got me a Fitbit Zip to keep track of how many calories I am burning and how many steps I am taking a day. It's pretty cool and I love it. Anyway, I was hoping I would drop the weight before we left for Utah/Idaho but I just can't seem to drop the last few pounds! That's okay though. My body hasn't been this active in a long time. And I can fit into a lot of my old clothes. I don't care to get back to my weight before I got married. I was so skinny back then. And it was before I had kids. After kids, my body is completely different but I am okay with that. Luckily I never wear a bikini!
