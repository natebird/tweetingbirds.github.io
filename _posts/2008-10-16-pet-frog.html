My mom found this sandbox for Kenzie at a yard sale. Unwilling to purchase sand at the stores, we let her play in the sandbox with some dirt she dug from our backyard. Yes, I know, we're so cheap. It's so hard to justify purchasing sand when I grew up with free sand at the beach just across the street (in Hawaii)! Anyway, we forgot to put the cover back on the sandbox when it rained two days in a row a week ago. Now we have a pond in our backyard and just as Nate was about to dump out the water, he saw a little frog.

Kenzie had a pet frog for a day, it disappeared the next morning. She had so much fun putting leaves and rocks in the "pond" for the frog. And she wanted to give the frog a house to live in, so she got her toy house and tried to put it in the pond for her new pet. She LOVES animals. She was so sad when the frog left the next day.
