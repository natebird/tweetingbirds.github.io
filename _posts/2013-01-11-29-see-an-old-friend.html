Old friend as in someone from my past, not old in age. Hehehe. For New Year we drove eight hours up to Kentucky to see our friends Taylor and Julia and their family. We love visiting them because they have three girls a few months younger in age as our girls. So all six girls have a blast playing together. Nate and I enjoy the company of Taylor and Julia. Nate and Taylor were roommates and best friends in college. They are two peas in a pod. Hilarious together.

Nate and I both made great college friends at BYU. We love that they knew us before we were married or had children. And it is fun to see all of us now married with children.

We make an effort to visit each other each year, sometimes Taylor and Julia come down and sometime we go up. They have a lovely house. And it just so happened that they received snow the weekend we were there and it was cold enough for it to keep on the ground. The girls were excited about that. And we always eat so good there because both Taylor and Julia are amazing cooks.
