I went to the thrift store and found a bunch of children's book there. I love purchasing children's book from thrift stores, it's so much cheaper! Anyway, one of the books was "Tumble Bumble" by Felicia Bond. I was telling Nate about it.

Tiffany: Yeah, I got a book by Felicia Bond. I think she's the author of those "Give a Mouse a Cookie" books.

Kenzie: [In an instant] No. No she's not. Laura Numeroff is the author of "If You Give a Mouse a Cookie."

Tiffany: Oh. I guess maybe Felicia Bond is the illustrator?

Kenzie: Yup. Felicia Bond illustrated the book.

I didn't realized I would be having such intelligent conversation with my 5-year-old. She knows her stuff.
