At Barron's funeral, my dad started talking to some of their riding friends about doing a memorial ride on Barron's birthday. Barron's birthday was Jan. 31. I put it out there on social media and made some cupcakes for the riders. What's a birthday party without cake?! I also showed up with my camera for pictures. We hope to do this every year. What a way to celebrate Barron's birthday!
