This was the second time we attended the GPB family day. This year it went a lot better. Less of a crowd because they limited the amount of people who could attend. The girls got to meet Curious George and get a picture with him. I didn't bring my camera so this is from the phone. We didn't stick around to meet Cat in the Hat.

They also got their face painted. Which they both loved. Laney sat really still. The most still she has ever sat. They also had a few other activities like coloring, painting, making play dough and such. They had fun.

We also went to Ikea. Twice. Before and after the family day event. We got there in time for their $1 breakfast. Fed the whole family breakfast for $6. And then we came back to do some shopping for some stuff for the girls' room and eat lunch. I love Ikea food. It's delicious and they had a great deal on lunch that we got food for the 4 of us for $10. The kids had fun playing around and going through all their stuff.

And then we ended the day with this. How great is that?
