Last night we ate delicious Chinese food at my parents' house with my grandparents, Robby, Lorna, and Marlane. Lorna and Marlane are visiting our family here and staying with my grandparents. Kenzie had a grand ol' time eating with chopsticks, playing with everyone, and having a fake picnic. She loves going over to Puo Puo and Dudepa's house. When we told her we were going yesterday morning, she kept asking about when we were leaving all day long. And on the way home, she kept talking about Puo Puo, picnic, Gi Gi, great grandpa, Robby, Dudepa, Marlane, Lorna, baby, hat, eating food, playing toys, until she fell asleep.

Eating dinner with chopsticks. My mom taught her to use chopsticks and she was able to get a few bites in her mouth.

My mom has this "My First Picnic" basket with material stuffed fake food in it to make a sandwich, a pint of milk, some fruit, and chicken legs. Kenzie loves going over to Puo Puo's house to play with the picnic. She is even all dressed up with a hat and apron.
