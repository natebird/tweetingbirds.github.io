Kenzie: Mom, don't tell me not the jump on the couch. I'm just going to pretend to jump on the couch.
[Kenzie gets up on the couch and gently bounces on it.]
Tiffany: That looks like jumping to me.
Kenzie: I'm just pretending to jump. [Then actually jumping up and down on the couch] Now I'm jumping on the couch.
