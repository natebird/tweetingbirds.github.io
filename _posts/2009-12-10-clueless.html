This was way too cute, I had to post about this even though it'll embarrass Nate a little. Last Friday while I was at the YW sleepover, I received a phone call from Nate. He usually doesn't call me while I'm doing church stuff unless it was an emergency or he had a question about the kids. So when he called, I answered. The YW had gone downstairs to the basement to hang out without the adults and I was just about to leave.

Tiffany: Hey honey, what's going on?
Nate: I was just wondering what do I need to do to put the sheets in the washer?
Tiffany: What? I don't get what you are asking. [Because I'm the one who does all the laundry in our household.]
Nate: How do I wash our sheets?
Tiffany: [still puzzled] Why are you trying to do the laundry?
Nate: So I can make our bed.
Tiffany: Umâ€¦ You put the dirty sheets in the washer and put detergent in it then turn it on.
Nate: How much detergent do I put in there?
Tiffany: Half a cup. You know, we do have another set of clean sheets.
Nate: Really? Where is it?
Tiffany: In the linen closet in our bathroom.
Nate: Oh. OK. [laughing at himself] And don't tell anyone about this.
Tiffany: Don't worry, Sherry is only standing right here next to me. [We're both laughing.]

Sherry is one of the YW's moms and our Relief Society President in our ward.
