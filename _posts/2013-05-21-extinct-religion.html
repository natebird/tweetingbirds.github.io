A conversation between Kenzie and Laney on the way home from school.

Kenzie: Today we learned about dart frogs. They are almost extinct.

Laney: What does extinct mean?

Kenzie: That means they are gone from the earth. Like dinosaurs.

Laney: Like Jesus?

Kenzie: No, not like Jesus because he could come down any time and visit us.

Laney: [Yelling out loud] Hey, Jesus! Come visit us!

Kenzie: It doesn't work like that. You have to pray like Joseph Smith.
