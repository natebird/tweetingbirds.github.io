This year's gingerbread contest was just our family. We kept it simple. I made potato soup for dinner. It was super yummy from Pioneer Woman's recipes. This year a lot of our gingerbread houses were challenged by gravity. Kept falling down. My dad was putting several graham crackers together to make the sidings of his house and Lai Lai said, "Dudepa, yours look like a dam!" We all laughed and wondered how she knew about a dam. This was Lai Lai's first year making gingerbread house. She liked decorating and licking all the candies and eating frosting. It was very cute.
