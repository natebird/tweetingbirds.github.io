I snatched these cute pictures from my dad's laptop. My parents take every opportunity to take cute pictures of Kenzie. And I finally got some off their computer. Some of these go back to September 2007.

This was taken Sept. 3, 2007. About 4 months ago Kenzie still looked like a little baby, just learning to walk.


She is playing with my mom's drawer of hair ties.


First she took them all out and now she is putting them all back in, one of her favorite past times.


Fine dining at a Chinese restaurant having dim sum. That was her first dim sum experience.


Her cousin Beau's first Sunday back from his mission to Guatemala.


When my dad and I stood in line for an hour or two waiting to test drive the Smart car, my mom took Kenzie to Whole Foods. This is in October.


Posing with the pumpkins.


Picking out her favorite one.


Resting on a pumpkin.


Shopping with my mom at JoAnn's and she put hair bands with bells on it in her hair. Look at her big eyes!


Kenzie loves to take time to smell the roses, even at Costco.


Other Costco customers were enjoying watching her smell the flowers.


The fake puppy chasing Kenzie at my parents' house.


Playing beauty shop with her Puo Puo (my mom).


Making a funny face at the ABC for lunch.


A self feeder slurping her cream of broccoli soup.


At my parents' ward Christmas party. It was a pajama party reenacting Christmas morning.


Playing beauty shop again with her Puo Puo, this time at our house.


I love how her hair naturally flips out. It gets little curly when it is wet.


Eyeing all the candy on the table, plotting a way to get to it... too bad it was for decorating the gingerbread houses.


Helping uncle Robby start out his gingerbread house. Robby was giving her pieces of graham crackers and she was shoving the whole piece in her mouth. He was quite amazed at how much she could fit in her mouth.


The finished product of my mom's gingerbread house.


Making a "birthday cake" for her Dudepa (my dad). It was my dad's birthday (the same night we made gingerbread houses).


Long ago she figured out how to stick her twist crayons into the screw holes on the bottom of her piano to make a birthday cake.


Going down her slide and into her ball pit. She loves doing that all day long. Then putting her crayons down the slide, and her puppy, and anything else she could find.


A cute little girl with pig tails. I'm glad she finally lets me do stuff with her hair!


Giving mom a hug from behind.


Playing with Dudepa!
