This is the reason why I haven't posted in a while and I probably won't post much until the games are over. I have to keep up with my brackets and watch the games. In my Real Simple magazine, it said employers lose about $1.2 million in productivity during March Madness as people fill out their brackets and keep tract of their standings. Kenzie wasn't quite sure what was happening with the basketball games until I pulled out our basketball. And now she plays along, bouncing or kicking it.
