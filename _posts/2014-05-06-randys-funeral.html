Randy's funeral was on Tuesday, April 1. That was the earliest the funeral home had available. It worked out great because even though Cyndi and Nicole had a lot of it planned ahead of time, there were still a lot to do once we actually knew what date it was. It was also spring break in Wenatchee so some of their friends were already out of town for the funeral. It was amazing how everyone worked together and was able to put together something in just a few days.

The funeral was so great. Nate gave the life sketch of his dad and it was very sweet. It was good closure for all of us. The program went well. And the day was beautiful.
