Kenzie's little Flounder has been missing for the last few days. I have searched everywhere in the house and all over her playroom for it - nothing. Today, he showed up out of nowhere while Kenzie was playing with it and her Disney princesses. At this point, I was curious to know where she found that little guy.

Tiffany: Kenzie, where did you find Flounder?
Kenzie: [nonchalant] He was swimming under the sea.
