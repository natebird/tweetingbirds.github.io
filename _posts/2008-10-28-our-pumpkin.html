I found the cutouts for Super Why characters to decorate our pumpkin with this year. For our Sunday activity, Nate and I helped Kenzie cut out and tape the pieces onto the pumpkin. The whole time she kept saying, "I'm putting decorations on my pumpkin!" Kenzie was very excited to have her favorite characters' faces on our pumpkins. Luckily, the pumpkin was big enough to put all four faces on there. She looks at it everyday and points out that Alpha Pig, Princess Presto, Wonder Red, and Super Why are on her pumpkin.

It's nice that the pieces are taped on there because I can take it off and actually cook the pumpkin this year. Previous years, we just threw our pumpkin in the woods but this year, I'm determined to do something with the pumpkin - pies, bread, etc. If anyone has pumpkin recipes or ideas on what to do with a pumpkin, let me know.
