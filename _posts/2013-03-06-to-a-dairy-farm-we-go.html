Another Groupon-type deal where I got tickets to a local dairy farm. Kenzie had been there before in kindergarten for a field trip, but the rest of us hadn't. The kids had a great time. We had a hay ride, fed animals, and watched how they milked cows. They are not a commercial dairy farm any more. Mostly just does tours and seasonal festival and activities. In the summer they have a little farmer's market there and I would go get cheap vegetables and local honey.
