Last Friday I started Laney on solids, more like liquid rice cereal on a spoon. She turned 5 months on Sunday. She took it well. At first her tongue didn't know what to do, but she quickly learned to swallow it.

She had been sleeping 9-10 hours before. But that night, after having solids for lunch and dinner, she slept from 7 p.m. to 3:45 a.m. I let her whine and fell back asleep at 4:30 then slept until 8:45. We had to wake her up at 8:45 because I was leaving for the youth temple trip at 9, or else she would have slept longer.
Speaking of sleep, she likes to sleep on her tummy. We put her in bed on her back, but she quickly flips over to her tummy. I tried to turn her back over but it disturbed her sleep then got mad and flipped back onto her tummy. She tosses and turns in her sleep like her mom.
