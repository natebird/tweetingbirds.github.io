Saturday morning we were going through the list of household chores we needed to get done after a week of neglect due to our birthday celebrations. But then I realized that it was the first full weekend of the month. That meant we could get into some museums and the zoo for free. Since the forecast called for rain, we chose something indoor. And off we went, leaving household chores behind for yet another day.

We went to the natural history museum. Neither one of us had been there before and it was such an amazing place. Extremely children friendly with lots of stuff to keep them occupied for hours. We ended up staying at the museum for four hours. At the end, Nate and I were too tired to keep going while Kenzie wanted to play some more. We happened to show up on a day when they had family fun day with archeologists. Laney also did well during the four hour of family fun at the museum. I have a feeling we will be back to the museum again.
