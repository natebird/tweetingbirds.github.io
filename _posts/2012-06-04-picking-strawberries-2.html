We love to go strawberry picking each year. Last year we didn't make it with the weather. So we were sure to make it this year. It's a drive to get out there but the strawberries are so good and it's a fun family thing we like to do together. They also have awesome strawberry frozen yogurt that's the perfect treat after a day of picking.

We also picked extra strawberries so I can make strawberry jam. It was my first year making it and it turned out pretty well. Hopefully it's enough jam to last us all year until we can go again next year.
