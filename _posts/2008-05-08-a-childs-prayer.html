This morning, I heard Kenzie say this little prayer in her bed through the baby monitor.

"Puo Puo. Maggie. Dudepa. Daddy working. Baby Bop. Night Night. AMEN!"

Translation:
Bless…
Puo Puo- my mom.
Maggie- my mom's friend who came to visit a couple months ago from Taiwan.
Dudepa- my dad.
Daddy working- Nate at work.
Baby Bop- her Baby Bop doll she got from aunt Lorna and Marlane.
Night Night- her security blanket
AMEN!- she says amen like she's from the South. It's a drawn out AAAAA-Men full of attitude and emphasis.

I love it! She is usually pretty good about folding her arms, bowing her head, and telling people to "Shhh!" during prayer. However, I wasn't able to peek in on her to see if she was folding her arms during her own prayer.
