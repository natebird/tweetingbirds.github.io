I will be turning 30 at the end of next April, about 6 months from now. I decided to make a list of things I wanted to do before I hit the big 3-0. Wow. 30 always seemed so old to me. But I'm feeling better about it as I inch closer to my next birthday. So here's my list, not in any particular order.

  1. Eat sushi. I love sushi and haven't had it in forever!
  2. Read the Book of Mormon entirely again.
  3. Lose the last 5 pounds of weight to get me to my weight before I had kids.
  4. Travel somewhere I have never been before.
  5. Make myself a skirt.
  6. Catch up on family blog. As you can tell, I'm so behind.
  7. Make ice cream using my ice cream maker. Bought one, still haven't used it yet.
  8. Read a book. I love to read, but I haven't read a book, cover-to-cover, in a long time because I've been so busy.
  9. Get my hair cut. I'm going to let it grow out first then chop it off in the spring.
  10. Write a list of memorable moments from the last 30 years of my life.
  11. Sleep in past 8 one morning.
  12. Make a new friend.
  13. Go to a sports game.
  14. Eat at a local restaurant that I haven't tried before.
  15. Buy something nice for myself, something that I want and not something I need.
  16. Take a class. Learn something new or improve upon a skill.
  17. Talk to a non-member about the church.
  18. Bake bread from scratch.
  19. Spend one-on-one time with each daughter.
  20. Go on a nice date with Nate.
  21. Help someone in need.
  22. Write down my testimony.
  23. Take an entire day off. Probably to catch up on family blog.
  24. Go to a concert or a show/play.
  25. Gather five Chinese recipes from mom.
  26. Interview my parents.
  27. Interview Nate's parents.
  28. Have a girls night out, going out and doing something fun.
  29. See an old friend. Old not in age but someone from my past.
  30. Go to the temple and doing a session.

P.S. For my birthday, rather than a cake I want the apple pie from Whole Foods. Yummy!
