Kenzie takes our Jenga pieces to build towers, which was a great opportunity for me to teach her to say "tall" and "high." She is definitely getting good at her hand-eye coordination. As she stacks the Jenga pieces, if the tower falls, she says, "Uh oh," and tries again. If she successfully adds another piece on top of the tower, she celebrates by saying, "Yay!" and giving herself a hand as shown in the last picture.
