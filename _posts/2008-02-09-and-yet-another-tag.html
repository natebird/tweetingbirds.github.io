What I was doing 10 years ago...
I was 14 years old and just moved to Georgia from Hawaii. I hated being in Georgia and leaving all my friends in Hawaii. I couldn't understand what people were saying with their Southern draw. I was in the small Cartersville Ward before it split into Cartersville and Cassville wards and before it became a stake. I was best friends with my cousin, Joycelyn, we did everything together, even went to the same high school. I missed Hawaii a lot. And this was also my freshman year in high school, the year before I got some fashion sense knocked into me.
