Last Friday, I told Kenzie that we were planning on spending some family time on Saturday morning going to the zoo for a family outing. Since Nate is gone four nights a week for school, Kenzie has noticed even more Mommy's night lately. Causing her to request more family time with both Mommy and Daddy.

Tiffany: Kenzie, what animals are we going to see at the zoo?
Kenzie: A pig. I love pigs. They are pink.
Tiffany: I don't think they have a pig at the zoo.
Kenzie: Yeah, they live in the barn.
Tiffany: So what animals will we see at the zoo?
Kenzie: Marty, Alex, Melman, and Gloria!

Yes, she has seen the movie, Madagasca, quite a few times.
