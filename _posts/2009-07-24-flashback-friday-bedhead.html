One of the fun things about getting Laney when she awakes is finding the state of her hair. I LOVE that mop on her head. I think it's so fitting to her personality, spunky. Her hair had not been altered in the pictures. It is what it is and she got it from her mother. (Embarrassing photo time.)

It all began with me. All I needed was some gold necklaces and I could have passed for Mr. T. And no, my mom didn't shave my head on the sides. And yes, I can hear you laughing. I was an ugly duckling waiting to blossom.
I passed the funky hair gene to Laney.
P.S. Little Houdini strikes again. She has recently figured out how to pull her sleeve out of her sewed long-sleeve onesie.
