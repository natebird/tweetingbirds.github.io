Kenzie: Mom, have you ever been to the circus?

Tiffany: No. I don't like the circus.

Kenzie: Why?

Tiffany: Because I don't like clowns.

Kenzie: But clowns are not scary. They are funny.

Tiffany: I don't think they are funny. I think they are scary.

Kenzie: Mom, you have to learn to control yourself about clowns. You should wish for clowns for Christmas so you can see that they are funny and not scary.
